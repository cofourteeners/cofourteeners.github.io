Class

1 - Easy hiking
2 - Moderate hiking
3 - Un-roped climbing
4 - Roped climbing
5 - Technical climbing

Exposure

1 - None; gentle terrain nearby
2 - Mild exposure nearby, none along route
3 - Mild exposure close to route; limited route options
4 - Moderate exposure along route; scrambling may be required
5 - Serious exposure; short technical moves required
6 - Dangerous exposure; steep drops and careful technical moves required
7 - Dangerous exposure; vertical cliffs with roped, technical climbing required

Road Difficulty

1 - 2WD; paved road
2 - 2WD; easy dirt road
3 - 2WD; rough dirt road
4 - 4WD; good clearance recommended
5 - 4WD; good clearance required, short wheel-based recommended
6 - 4WD; high clearance required, short wheel-based required
7 - 4WD; modified 4WD vehicle required

Prominence

Defined as the minimum elevation required to descend from one summit to another that is taller

Isolation

Defined as distance to nearest point of equal elevation
